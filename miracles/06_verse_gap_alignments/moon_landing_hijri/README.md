## Addressing Criticism

**"Why Hijri calendar, not Gregorian?"**

- The Quran's community measures time by lunar Hijri calendar
- The number 1,389 naturally corresponds to a Hijri year
- Not "choosing" Hijri - observing what the number represents

**"Moon split = historical miracle, not moon landing"**

- Classical interpretation refers to a miracle during Prophet's time
- Arabic perfect tense allows prophetic/future certainty readings
- The numerical alignment exists independently of interpretation
- We're observing the structural pattern, not replacing classical exegesis

**"Could verse structure be manipulated?"**

- Quran's structure fixed in 7th century
- Verse order universally agreed upon
- No one in 7th century could know:
  - Humans would land on the moon
  - When it would happen
  - What 1389 AH would represent
