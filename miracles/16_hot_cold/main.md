## Why Hail Is Excluded: Linguistic Analysis

### Different Semantic Categories

**Temperature concepts (abstract):**

- حَرّ (harr) = heat (the quality of being hot)
- بَرْد (bard) = coldness (the quality of being cold)
- بَارِد (barid) = cool (having the quality of coldness)
- حَرُور (harur) = scorching heat (intense heat quality)

**Precipitation phenomena (concrete):**

- بَرَد (barad) = hail (frozen rain pellets, ice falling from sky)
- مَطَر (matar) = rain (water falling from sky)
- ثَلْج (thalj) = snow (frozen precipitation)

### The Quran's Own Usage Confirms This Distinction

**بَرْدًا (coolness) - Temperature Context:**

**Verse 21:69:**

> قُلْنَا يَٰنَارُ كُونِى بَرْدًا وَسَلَٰمًا عَلَىٰٓ إِبْرَٰهِيمَ

> "We said, 'O **fire**, be **coolness** and safety upon Abraham.'"

**Analysis:**

- Context: FIRE becoming COOL (pure temperature transformation)
- No weather/precipitation mentioned
- Abstract quality (coolness) applied to fire
- Semantic domain: Temperature/thermal properties

**بَرَدٍ (hail) - Precipitation Context:**

**Verse 24:43:**

> أَلَمْ تَرَ أَنَّ ٱللَّهَ يُزْجِى سَحَابًا ثُمَّ يُؤَلِّفُ بَيْنَهُۥ ثُمَّ يَجْعَلُهُۥ رُكَامًا فَتَرَى ٱلْوَدْقَ يَخْرُجُ مِنْ خِلَٰلِهِۦ وَيُنَزِّلُ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مِن جِبَالٍ فِيهَا مِنۢ بَرَدٍ

> "Do you not see that Allah drives **clouds**? Then He brings them together, then He makes them into a mass, and you see the **rain** emerge from within it. And He sends down from the sky, mountains [of clouds] within which is **hail**...The flash of its **lightning**..."

**Analysis:**

- Context: CLOUDS, RAIN, HAIL, LIGHTNING (weather phenomena)
- Hail grouped with precipitation events
- Concrete objects falling from sky
- Semantic domain: Meteorology/weather
